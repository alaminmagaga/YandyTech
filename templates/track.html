Digital Technology & Innovation Lab
(DigiLabs)

DigiLabs is an initiative of YandyTech localising & supporting young people with Tech Education and Mentorship to
prepare the next billion creators and users for the future jobs.

Overview

DigiLabs, the tech education and mentorship initiative of YandyTech Community. Our mission is to empower the next generation of tech, creators, users and leaders by providing them with the tools, skills, and guidance they need to succeed in the digital age. We believe that everyone should have access to quality tech education, regardless of their background or socioeconomic status.




Projects

At DigiLabs, we offer a range of projects to help aspiring tech leaders reach their full potential. Our programs include:

Coding Bootcamps

Our coding bootcamps are designed to teach you the fundamentals of programming, from basic syntax to complex algorithms. Our experienced instructors will guide you through hands-on projects and provide personalised feedback to help you succeed.


Data Bootcamps

Our Data Bootcamp program is designed for individuals who want to develop their skills in data analysis, data visualisation, and data-driven decision-making. Over the course of the program, participants will learn how to:

  • Collect, clean, and analyse data using popular data analysis tools such as Python, R, and SQL
  • Create compelling visualisations and dashboards to communicate insights to stakeholders.
  • Use data to inform strategic decision-making in a variety of industries, from healthcare to finance.

The Data Bootcamp is a hands-on, project-based program that provides participants with real-world experience working with data. Our experienced instructors will guide you through the program and provide personalised feedback to help you succeed.

Upon completion of the Data Bootcamp, participants will have developed the skills and knowledge needed to pursue a career in data analysis, business intelligence, or related fields.

Mentorship Programs

Our mentorship programs pair you with experienced tech professionals who can help you navigate the industry and provide advice on everything from career paths to networking.

Why DigiLabs?

At YandyTech, we understand that young people faces challenges of unemployment, underemployment and staying dedicated, committed and disciplined
when navigating the into tech due to lack of curated resources and mentorship.
