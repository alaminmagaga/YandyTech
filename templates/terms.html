2.1 “Donation” means any such money as may be given in contribution to YandyTech to further its objectives.

2.2 “Legal Member” means any young person, man or woman who may at any time become a part of YandyTech Community or team.

4.0 Legal Member

4.1 A legal member is any young person, man or woman who via YandyTech’s membership application form puts in an application, and upon review of such application is accepted.

4.2 A legal member shall for the purpose of her membership to any of YandyTech’s platforms, projects or programmes, be subject to all YandyTech’s rules and regulations until at any such time when his/her membership may terminate.

4.3 Where a legal member is assigned a position, she shall, subject to YandyTech’s guidelines for such a position, carry out his/her functions for the sole purpose of furthering YandyTech’s legal objectives.

4.4 A legal member may at any time communicate in writing to YandyTech of her decision to have his/her membership terminated.
